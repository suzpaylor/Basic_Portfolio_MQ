Let's go back to 1984 and the very first version of MacPaint on an original Macintosh PC. I spent hours on this program creating all sorts of creatures, completely mesmorized with the technology! Fast-foward 15 years, to my first 'real' job at a Molecular Genetics lab. The work was always exciting... but to be honest I found the most satisfaction in teaching myself how to take images from a microscope to Adobe Photoshop for publication. Through lots of trial and error I became the local Photoshop expert. Since that time, the kitchen has become my laboratory and home of the Bonneville Bakery and my favorite recipes are recorded on suzdaily.com. I love spending time with my family - baking, decorating, skiing, gardening, hiking and enjoying all that these beautiful mountains have to offer. I am beyond excited to break-up with WordPress, finish this UofU Bootcamp strong and re-enter the workforce with the skills of a Web Developer.
